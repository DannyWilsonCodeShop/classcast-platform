# Lesson Modules - User Guide

## For Instructors

### Getting Started

#### 1. Access Lesson Modules
- Login to your instructor account
- You'll see a personalized greeting on your dashboard (e.g., "Good morning, John!")
- Click the purple **"Modules"** button (ðŸ“š) in the top navigation bar

#### 2. Create Your First Module

**Step 1: Click "Create Module"**
- On the Lesson Modules page, click the white "Create Module" button in the header
- Or click "Create Your First Module" if you have no modules yet

**Step 2: Module Setup**
- Enter a **Module Title** (e.g., "Introduction to Calculus")
- Write a **Description** explaining what students will learn
- Select the **Course** this module belongs to
- Click "Next"

**Step 3: Add Intro Video (Optional)**
- Paste a YouTube or Google Drive video URL
- This video introduces students to the module
- You can skip this and add it later
- Click "Next"

**Step 4: Review & Create**
- Review all your information
- Click "Create Module"
- You'll be redirected to the module editor

#### 3. Add Lesson Videos

**From the Module Editor:**
- Click the **"Lessons"** tab
- Click **"Add Lesson"** button
- Fill in:
  - **Lesson Title** (e.g., "Understanding Limits")
  - **Description** (what students will learn)
  - **Video URL** (YouTube or Google Drive)
  - **Duration** in minutes
- Click "Add Lesson"

**Tips:**
- Add lessons in the order you want students to watch them
- Each lesson can have interactive questions (coming in Phase 2)
- Use clear, descriptive titles

#### 4. Organize Your Module

**Reorder Lessons:**
- Use the up/down arrow buttons to change lesson order
- Lessons are numbered automatically

**Edit Lessons:**
- Click the pencil icon to edit lesson details
- Update title, description, or video URL

**Delete Lessons:**
- Click the trash icon to remove a lesson
- Confirm the deletion

#### 5. Publish Your Module

**When Ready:**
- Click the green **"Publish"** button in the module header
- Students can now access the module
- The status badge changes from "Draft" to "Published"

**To Unpublish:**
- Click **"Unpublish"** to hide from students
- Make changes while unpublished
- Republish when ready

### Module Management

#### Edit Module Details
- Click **"Edit Details"** in the module header
- Update title, description, or intro video
- Click "Save Changes"

#### View Module Stats
On the Overview tab, see:
- Total number of lessons
- Number of interactive questions
- Students enrolled

#### Delete a Module
- From the modules list, click the trash icon
- Confirm deletion
- **Warning:** This cannot be undone!

### Best Practices

#### Creating Effective Modules

1. **Clear Structure**
   - Start with an intro video explaining the module
   - Break content into 10-15 minute lessons
   - Use sequential ordering (basics â†’ advanced)

2. **Engaging Content**
   - Use high-quality videos
   - Keep lessons focused on one topic
   - Add interactive questions (Phase 2)

3. **Student Success**
   - Write clear descriptions
   - Set realistic expectations
   - Provide practice tests (Phase 2)

#### Video Guidelines

**Recommended:**
- YouTube videos (public or unlisted)
- Google Drive videos (shared with "Anyone with link")
- 720p or higher resolution
- Clear audio quality

**Avoid:**
- Private videos students can't access
- Very long videos (break into parts)
- Poor audio or video quality

## Troubleshooting

### Video Not Displaying
- Check that the URL is correct
- Ensure video is public or unlisted (YouTube)
- Verify sharing settings (Google Drive)
- Try a different browser

### Can't Create Module
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Check that you've selected a course
- Verify you're logged in as instructor

### Lessons Not Saving
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure video URL is valid
- Try refreshing the page
- Contact support if issue persists

### Module Not Appearing for Students
- Check that module is **Published** (not Draft)
- Verify students are enrolled in the course
- Wait a few minutes for changes to sync
